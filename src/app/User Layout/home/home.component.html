About FoodHub

FoodHub is a smart food guide that brings multiple restaurants together in one place. It allows users to explore different restaurants, browse their menus, and compare meals easily — all without the need to visit each restaurant separately.

The goal of FoodHub is to simplify the ordering decision process by providing a clear, organized view of available options in one convenient platform. Whether you’re looking for a quick bite or a full meal, FoodHub helps you choose faster and smarter.
